About Me

My name is John Lins, I'm a developer and student from the Bay Area, CA. I currently attend UCSB as a computer engineering (CpE) major ω/ a mathematics minor!
I build math/physics/ML simulations among other things, but I aspire to involve myself in seq-2-seq transformer projects/research in the future.
I'm quite active in the CS/ML field... I'm the past president of my school's Hack Club, additionally I'm the lead organizer @ Sigmoid Hacks.
My "focus" project is Loyae.com, which aims to unleash the power of natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning for website optimization.
